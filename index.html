About Me

I am Chenye Wang, an M.Eng. student in Electronic and Information Engineering at Fudan University, under the supervision of Prof. Yi Jiang and Prof. Xin Wang.

I received my B.Eng. degree in Communication Engineering from Chongqing University of Post and Telecommunications in 2023.

I will join the Charles L. Brown Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at University of Virginia as a Ph.D. student, advised by Prof. Cong Shen.

My research interests include signal processing, wireless communications, AI/ML, and federated learning.
